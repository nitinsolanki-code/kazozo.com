Subprocessors

Last updated: April 15, 2026

Kazozo engages a small set of third-party vendors ("subprocessors") to help deliver our service. These vendors process customer data strictly under our instruction, under written agreements that require them to meet data-protection and security standards at least as strong as ours.

Notification of changes

We notify customers at least 30 days before engaging a new subprocessor or materially changing the role of an existing one. Customers can subscribe to subprocessor updates by emailing legal@kazozo.com with the subject "Subscribe to subprocessor updates."

If a customer has a reasonable, documented objection to a new subprocessor (for example, a compliance conflict), they may raise it during the notice period and we'll work in good faith to resolve it — up to and including an orderly exit if no alternative exists.

International data transfers

Where Kazozo or a subprocessor processes personal data of individuals in the EEA, UK, or Switzerland, transfers to the United States are made under the EU Standard Contractual Clauses (2021/914), the UK International Data Transfer Addendum, and — where applicable — the EU–US Data Privacy Framework. The current list of active frameworks is maintained as part of our DPA.

Questions about this list? Email legal@kazozo.com.
