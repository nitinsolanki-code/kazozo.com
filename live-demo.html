Frequently Asked Questions

What counts as a "resolution"?
A resolution is one complete interaction an agent handles from start to finish — whether it takes one message or ten. Multi-turn conversations count as one resolution.
Why do agents have different prices?
Price reflects value delivered. An FAQ Bot answering "what are your hours?" is simpler than a Proposal Writer drafting a $5K contract. You pay based on what each agent does for you.
Can I start with just one agent?
Yes. Pick any single agent and pay per resolution. Add more agents anytime — each one is independent. No bundles or packages required.
What happens when you add new agents?
New agents are available immediately at their listed price. Your existing agents and pricing are never affected. It's like adding an app — your other apps don't change.
Is there a minimum?
Each active agent has a small monthly minimum: $15/mo for Utility agents, $25/mo for Workflow agents, $50/mo for Strategic agents. You pay the greater of your minimum or actual usage. Most active users clear the minimum within the first week.
What about setup?
Setup is included. We connect your CRM, configure your agents, and go live in 1-2 weeks. No SI partner required — Salesforce implementations typically cost $50-200K.
