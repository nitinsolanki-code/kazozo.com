Pricing & Billing
An AI response is a single reply generated by the AI assistant. Your plan includes a monthly allocation of AI responses. A customer conversation spanning multiple back-and-forth exchanges will use multiple AI responses. Automated workflow-only replies that do not involve the AI model are not counted toward your allocation. Overages are billed at your plan's per-AI-response rate.
