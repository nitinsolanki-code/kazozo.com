End-to-end

Agents work together, not in silos

The real power is when multiple agents handle a workflow from start to finish.

📞
Injury to Treatment Plan Enrollment
  • Patient calls with acute pain/injury
  • Frontline Agent captures symptoms, urgency, treatment history
  • Scheduler Agent books same-day or next-day urgent appointment
  • Qualifier Agent pre-screens health history before visit
  • Dr. evaluates, prescribes care plan
  • Sales Agent presents plan, gets commitment, schedules all visits
💪
Multi-Week Care Plan Compliance
  • Patient starts Week 1 of care plan (e.g., 2x/week for 4 weeks)
  • Content Agent sends home exercises immediately post-visit
  • Support Agent monitors exercises, sends motivation/tips
  • Scheduler Agent sends appointment reminders (avoid no-shows)
  • Support Agent does mid-plan check-in to ensure compliance
  • Patient completes plan with high success rate
🎯
Prevention & Maintenance Membership
  • Patient completes acute care plan (healed)
  • Sales Agent presents 'Wellness Membership': 1x/month preventive visits
  • Patient enrolls in monthly membership ($50-100/month recurring)
  • Scheduler Agent books monthly appointments automatically
  • Support Agent sends between-visit wellness tips, stretches
  • Patient stays engaged, prevents re-injury, becomes long-term revenue
📊
Treatment Outcome Tracking
  • Patient outcome data collected (pain level, range of motion, function)
  • Content Agent educates patient on healing timeline & expectations
  • Support Agent monitors outcomes over care plan
  • Report Agent calculates success rate, treatment time, revenue per protocol
  • Identifies most effective protocols (highest success rate + highest revenue)
  • Marketing and staffing adjusted based on data
🔄
Multi-Location Wellness Center Coordination
  • Patient books acupuncture at Location A
  • Qualifier + Sales agents pitch complementary services (chiro at Location B, PT at Location C)
  • Patient booked into multi-disciplinary care plan
  • Each location uses Content + Support agents for their modality
  • Report Agent tracks cross-location patient value, bundled package ROI
⭐
Retention Through Continuous Care
  • Patient completes primary care plan, pain resolved
  • Without follow-up, patient drifts away (80% re-injury rate)
  • Support + Scheduler agents proactively offer maintenance plan
  • Patient enrolls in monthly prevention membership
  • Lifetime value increases 5x (acute visit $150 vs. $50/month x 36 months = $1,800)
