End-to-end

Agents work together, not in silos

The real power is when multiple agents handle a workflow from start to finish.

📞
New Patient Inquiry to Consultation to Start
  • Frontline Agent Agent captures inquiry (HRT/TRT interest, basic symptoms, timeline)
  • Frontline Agent books consultation appointment
  • Lead Lead Qualifier pre-screens health history and realistic expectations
  • Patient attends consultation with Dr.
  • Sales Agent Agent explains treatment options and cost upfront
  • Patient starts treatment with clear expectations set by Content Agent Agent
💊
Ongoing Patient Management & Monitoring
  • Patient starts hormone therapy (injection, pill, patch, cream)
  • Content Agent Agent sends week-by-week adjustment expectations
  • Week 2: Customer Customer Support checks on side effects, normal or concerning
  • Week 4: Frontline Agent books lab work
  • Week 6: Customer Customer Support checks results, celebrates progress
  • Month 3: Quarterly review with Dr., potential dose adjustment
📊
Lab Management & Dose Optimization
  • Labs are due (12-week intervals typical)
  • Frontline Agent sends lab appointment options + consent forms
  • Labs completed at partner location (LabCorp/Quest)
  • Results arrive in chart within 48 hours
  • Weekly Weekly Report analyzes results vs. target ranges
  • Dr. reviews, Customer Customer Support communicates dose change or continuation to patient
💪
Patient Compliance & Retention
  • Monthly Customer Customer Support check-in on how patient is feeling
  • Celebrate milestones: energy up, symptoms down, labs improving
  • Address concerns: side effects, cost questions, medication adherence
  • Quarterly: Content Agent Agent sends updated education based on patient's stage
  • Annual: Comprehensive outcome review with Weekly Weekly Report, celebrate progress
🏥
Medication Refill & Prescription Management
  • Customer Customer Support monitors when refills are needed
  • Sends refill request to Dr. (or auto-approved for routine refills)
  • Pharmacy receives prescription, fills automatically
  • Patient receives reminder that refill is ready for pickup
  • Frontline Agent tracks fill dates and sends renewal notices before running out
🎯
Long-Term Outcome Tracking & Analytics
  • Weekly Weekly Report tracks all patients on each protocol monthly
  • Measures: symptom improvement, lab results, side effects, compliance, satisfaction
  • Identifies top-performing protocols and patients likely to refer
  • Quarterly report shows revenue per protocol, patient lifetime value, referral patterns
  • Informs marketing strategy and treatment recommendations
