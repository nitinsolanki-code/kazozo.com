Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 14, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you use Kazozo's website or AI agent platform, we may collect:

  • Contact information you provide (name, email, phone number, company name)
  • Conversation data from interactions with our AI agents
  • Usage data and analytics (pages visited, features used, session duration)
  • CRM data that you choose to connect through our integrations
  • Device and browser information for security and performance optimization

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve our AI agent services
  • Configure and deploy agents for your industry
  • Process transactions and send related communications
  • Respond to your requests and provide customer support
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve our platform
  • Send product updates and marketing communications (with your consent)

3. Data Protection

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including encryption in transit and at rest, access controls, and regular security audits. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

4. Data Sharing

We share data only with:

  • Service providers necessary to operate our platform (hosting, analytics, payment processing)
  • CRM and integration partners you've authorized
  • Law enforcement when required by law

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, delete, or export your personal data. For California residents (CCPA) and EU residents (GDPR), additional rights may apply. Contact us at privacy@kazozo.com to exercise your rights.

6. Cookies

We use essential cookies to operate our website and analytics cookies to understand usage. You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. After account closure, we delete your data within 90 days unless required by law to retain it longer.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. We'll notify you of material changes via email or a notice on our website.

9. Contact Us

Questions about privacy? Contact us at privacy@kazozo.com or through our AI agent by clicking "Talk to Kaz" on any page.
