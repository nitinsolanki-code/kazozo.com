Frequently Asked Questions

What counts as an AI response? +
One customer interaction that the agent handles end-to-end. For a support agent, that's one ticket close. For sales, one qualified lead. For HR, one PTO request. Not per message — per resolved task.
What's included in the Lead Qualifier price ($0.45/res)? +
All data provider calls, multi-source enrichment, BANT scoring, affluence calculation, CRM update, and the full research report. No hidden fees.
How does affluence scoring work? +
We pull revenue, company size, industry, and role data, then apply our proprietary model. You get a score 1–100 indicating likelihood they can afford your service. Customizable thresholds.
Can I start with just one agent? +
Yes. Every agent has a low monthly minimum ($15–$50). Spin up one, test it, scale when ready. No long-term contract required.
Which CRMs do you integrate with? +
All major CRMs — cloud and on-prem — plus custom REST APIs. If you use it, we integrate.
How long does setup take? +
Minutes for a basic agent — define workflows, test, go live. Our team helps with complex multi-agent setups.
What channels do your agents work on? +
Phone, email, SMS, web chat, Facebook Messenger, WhatsApp, Teams, Slack, and custom webhooks. One agent, every channel.
