End-to-end

Agents work together, not in silos

The real power is when multiple agents handle a workflow from start to finish.

📸
Social Media Inquiry to Surgery Pipeline
  • Patient DMs or comments on Instagram/TikTok about surgery
  • Frontline Agent responds in real-time, gathers procedure interest + location + timeline
  • Frontline Agent books consultation appointment
  • Lead Qualifier pre-screens health & expectations
  • Patient arrives at consultation, Sales Agent highlights before/afters that match patient goals
  • Post-surgery, Review Manager collects before/after testimonials
💰
Objection Handling & Financing Close
  • Patient loves results but hesitates on cost
  • Sales Agent proactively presents financing (CareCredit, payment plans)
  • Sales Agent calculates exact monthly payment in real-time
  • Patient agrees to financing, books pre-op appointment
  • Frontline Agent sends pre-op checklist and surgical prep instructions
📋
Pre-Surgery Qualification & Prep
  • Patient books consultation after Lead Qualifier screening
  • Frontline Agent books surgery date at consultation
  • 6 weeks pre-op: Frontline Agent sends prep timeline
  • 2 weeks pre-op: Lead Qualifier confirms health history, medications, allergies
  • 1 week pre-op: Content Agent sends detailed recovery expectations
  • 24 hours pre-op: Frontline Agent confirms arrival time, last-minute questions
⭐
Post-Op Follow-Up & Review Generation
  • Day 1 post-op: Content Agent sends pain management & activity restrictions
  • Week 2: Lead Qualifier checks on healing progress, reports concerns to Dr.
  • Week 6: Review Manager requests before/after testimonial + photos for marketing
  • Week 8: Final check-in, celebrate results, encourage referrals
  • Before/after gallery updated, drives 10%+ of future leads
🎯
Lead Attribution & Marketing ROI
  • Every inquiry tagged with source (Instagram, Google, referral, etc.)
  • Lead tracked through qualification, consultation, surgery decision
  • Surgery revenue attributed back to original marketing channel
  • Weekly Report generates monthly ROI by channel
  • Budget reallocated to highest-value channels (e.g., Google > Instagram)
