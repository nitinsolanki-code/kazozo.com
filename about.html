About Kazozo

A 20-year-old domain.
A brand new mission.

Kazozo is a word borrowed from Zambia. We bought the domain two decades ago without a plan. Then AI agents became real — and Kazozo found its purpose.

The name came first.

Over twenty years ago, we came across the word "kazozo" — a term used in Zambia — and bought the domain on instinct. It was short, memorable, and had energy. For a long time, it just sat there. A domain waiting for the right idea.

That idea arrived when AI agents matured enough to actually run business operations. Not chatbots that deflect questions — real agents that qualify leads, close deals, handle support tickets, and coordinate service delivery. The kind of capability that used to require a team of five, now packaged into software that works around the clock.

Kazozo became the platform to deliver that capability to businesses that can't afford a six-figure AI buildout but need enterprise-grade results.

What we build.

Kazozo is an AI operations platform. We deploy specialized agents — for sales, support, and operations — that work together as a coordinated team. They qualify leads, respond to customers in seconds, schedule appointments, manage pipelines, and generate real-time analytics.

Every agent is pre-configured for your industry. We're live across 35+ verticals, from home services to healthcare to hospitality. Most businesses are fully operational within one to two weeks.
