Context. Northwind Logistics wants to reduce manual order entry and inbound "where's my order" calls by introducing a customer self-serve portal and automating order creation from inbound email.

Goal. Cut rep re-keying and free support capacity, while respecting tiered pricing visibility for wholesale accounts and planning for German-market distributors.

Recommended next step. Confirm scope of v1 (portal status + email intake), resolve pricing-visibility rules, and agree a target go-live in the follow-up workshop.

Acceptance criteria
  • Inbound order email creates a draft order in the CRM
  • Quantities and SKUs are parsed and shown for confirmation
  • Rep confirms before the order is committed
US-02High
As a customer, I want live order status in a self-serve portal so I don't have to call support.
Acceptance criteria
  • Status updates within 5 minutes of a fulfilment change
  • Accessible via secure link without a full login
US-03Medium
As a sales manager, I want pricing hidden from wholesale-tier customers so account terms stay protected.
Acceptance criteria
  • Pricing visibility is driven by customer tier
  • Wholesale accounts see status but not list pricing
