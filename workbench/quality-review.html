What it checks

Six lenses on every requirement

Each item is scored and annotated, with concrete rewrite suggestions where it falls short.

🌫️

Ambiguity

Vague words like “fast”, “easy”, “robust” with no measurable meaning.

🕳️

Gaps

Missing acceptance criteria, edge cases, and error paths.

👯

Duplication

Overlapping or contradictory requirements across the set.

🔗

Traceability

Requirements with no clear source, owner, or linked objective.

✅

Testability

Whether each item can actually be verified as done.

📐

INVEST scoring

A 1–5 quality score per story, with the reasons behind it.

As a user, I want pages to load in under 2s at 500 concurrent users.
