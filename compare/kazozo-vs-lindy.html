Frequently Asked Questions

What agents does Kazozo include? ▼

Kazozo provides 5 core agents covering key business functions, plus 6 expansion agents for specialized use cases. Each agent is pre-configured for your specific industry vertical and comes with best practices, templates, and integrations ready to go. The agents handle lead qualification, customer support, appointment scheduling, data enrichment, and more—depending on your industry.

Can I customize agents in Kazozo? ▼

Yes. While Kazozo agents arrive pre-optimized for your industry, they're fully customizable. Your dedicated implementation team helps configure agents to your exact workflows, tone, and requirements. Unlike building from scratch, you start with industry best practices and modify from there—saving months of setup and optimization time.

How does Kazozo's AI response pricing work? ▼

Kazozo charges a base monthly fee ($249–$999) depending on your plan and industry vertical, plus a per-AI-response cost for each task the agent completes (e.g., per qualified lead, per resolved support ticket). This aligns costs with business impact—you pay more as your agents drive more value. Lindy.ai, by contrast, charges a flat monthly fee regardless of usage.

What industries does Kazozo serve? ▼

Kazozo specializes in 27 industry verticals including SaaS, Insurance, Real Estate, Healthcare, Professional Services, E-commerce, Staffing, Finance, Legal, Education, Hospitality, Manufacturing, and more. Each vertical gets industry-specific agents, workflows, compliance templates, and integrations. If your industry isn't listed, contact sales—we're continuously expanding verticals.

How quickly can I get Kazozo agents live? ▼

Kazozo is a done-for-you service. Most customers have agents live within 1–2 weeks. Your dedicated implementation team handles discovery, configuration, testing, training, and launch. Lindy.ai, by contrast, requires you to build agents yourself—typically 2–4 weeks or more depending on complexity and your technical skills.

Can both platforms integrate with CRM systems? ▼

Yes, both Kazozo and Lindy.ai support CRM integrations. Kazozo comes with pre-built, industry-standard CRM connectors (HubSpot, Salesforce, Pipedrive, etc.) configured for your vertical. Lindy.ai offers CRM integrations but you configure them yourself. Either way, agents can read/write data to your CRM seamlessly.

Is Lindy.ai cheaper than Kazozo? ▼

Lindy.ai has a lower entry cost ($49–$299/mo). However, Kazozo's higher price ($249–$999/mo + per AI response) includes dedicated onboarding, industry optimization, and done-for-you setup—meaning faster ROI and less internal effort. Calculate total cost of ownership: Lindy.ai's lower cost + your time investment vs. Kazozo's higher cost + faster launch + higher impact.

Which platform has better compliance & security? ▼

Both platforms prioritize security. Kazozo's agents are pre-configured with industry compliance best practices (HIPAA for healthcare, GDPR for EU, SOC 2 for enterprise, etc.). Lindy.ai is flexible and secure but requires you to configure compliance yourself. If your industry has strict compliance requirements, Kazozo's pre-built templates save significant time and risk.
