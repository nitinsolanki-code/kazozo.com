Who each one is for

They're not really competitors — they're tools for different problems. But if you're deciding which one should run your customer workflow, here's the honest split.

Pick Kazozo if…

You want AI to pick up your phone at 9pm, answer the website chat at 2am, qualify every lead, send the SMS follow-up, and drop the contact into your CRM. You want this running across 27 industries' worth of packaged agents without hiring an engineer. You care about logs, policies, and a single monthly number.

Stick with ChatGPT if…

You want a personal assistant to help you draft emails, clean up spreadsheets, summarize documents, and think out loud. ChatGPT is fantastic at that. What it isn't — out of the box — is a customer-facing system that captures leads, answers calls, and syncs to your CRM. Many Kazozo customers use both.

Questions we hear a lot

Can I just embed ChatGPT on my website and call it a day?
You can embed a chat window that talks to a language model — but a raw embed doesn't capture leads, doesn't know your pricing, doesn't sync to your CRM, doesn't follow up by SMS, and doesn't answer your phone. Those pieces are the actual business system. Kazozo is the hosted version of that system so you don't have to build it.
Does Kazozo use the same AI as ChatGPT?
Kazozo uses premium AI on every plan — the specific underlying model evolves as better options ship, which is exactly what you want. The bigger question isn't the model, it's what it's allowed to do, what data it has, and what actions it can take. That's the layer we've built.
What's an "AI agent" vs a chatbot?
A chatbot responds to the message in front of it. An agent has goals — qualify this lead, book this call, request this review, escalate to a human if it can't resolve. Kazozo's 11 agents each own a specific outcome and can hand off to each other. ChatGPT is single-threaded by design: one conversation, one response.
How much does Kazozo cost compared to ChatGPT Plus?
They aren't really comparable. ChatGPT Plus is $20/seat for individual productivity. Kazozo starts at $249/mo and replaces what would otherwise be a chatbot vendor + phone answering service + SMS tool + reputation management tool. See our pricing page for the full breakdown.
Is it safe to put a raw LLM in front of my customers?
Only if you've built prompt-injection defenses, output filters, audit logs, tenant isolation, and a policy layer — and have someone watching it. Kazozo is that layer. Every conversation is logged, every action is gated, and the agents operate inside explicit policies you control.
