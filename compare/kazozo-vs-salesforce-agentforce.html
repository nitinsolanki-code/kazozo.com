Frequently Asked Questions

Can Kazozo integrate with our existing CRM system? +

Yes! Kazozo integrates with popular CRM systems including Salesforce (if you're already using it), HubSpot, Pipedrive, and others via API. We also support custom integrations for specialized systems. Our team can help you set up the right integration for your tech stack.

How quickly can we go live with Kazozo? +

Most customers go live within 1-7 days. Our pre-built industry templates and simple setup process mean you're not waiting for custom development. For straightforward use cases, some clients launch in just 24-48 hours. We'll work with your team to ensure a smooth implementation.

What if we already have Salesforce? Should we use Agentforce instead? +

Not necessarily. If you're a smaller team or want to launch agents quickly without massive Salesforce investment, Kazozo is a great option. You can integrate with Salesforce while keeping costs low and time-to-value high. Many SMBs use Kazozo alongside Salesforce for faster, more cost-effective agent deployment.

What happens when we scale beyond our current plan? +

Kazozo is designed to scale with you. As your needs grow, you can add more agents, increase conversation volume, and unlock advanced features—all with transparent, predictable pricing. No surprise bills or forced enterprise contracts. Our growth plans support teams from 5 people to over 500.
