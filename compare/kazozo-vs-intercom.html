Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about switching from Intercom to Kazozo

Can Kazozo integrate with my existing Intercom data? +
Yes. We provide seamless migration tools to import your customer data, conversation history, and workflows from Intercom. Our team handles the migration process to ensure zero data loss and minimal disruption to your operations.
How does Kazozo's per-resolution pricing compare to Intercom's per-seat model? +
Intercom charges $39-99+ per team member monthly, costs grow with your team. Kazozo charges $149/month base fee plus per-resolution costs, typically 50-70% cheaper for teams handling 5,000+ monthly interactions. For example, a 10-person support team on Intercom costs $390-990/month; on Kazozo, the same volume costs $149 + resolution fees (usually $200-400).
Does Kazozo support human handoff to my team? +
Absolutely. Kazozo's AI agents can intelligently escalate complex issues to your human team with full conversation context. You maintain complete control over escalation rules and can monitor all agent-to-human transitions in real-time.
Can Kazozo handle our sales qualification and lead scoring? +
Yes, that's a core differentiator. Kazozo includes dedicated lead qualification and scoring agents that work 24/7. They qualify prospects, score by fit, and pass qualified leads directly to your sales team. Intercom requires additional tools or manual processes for this.
What's the onboarding process like? +
Kazozo typically deploys in 24-48 hours. We provide: initial setup consultation, data migration from existing tools, custom agent configuration for your workflows, integration testing, and training for your team. Our onboarding specialists guide you through every step.
Do you offer industry-specific configurations? +
Yes. Kazozo offers 27 industry-specific packages (SaaS, ecommerce, healthcare, real estate, etc.) with pre-configured agents, workflows, and compliance settings. This means faster deployment and better results for your specific business model.
Can I use Kazozo with my CRM or existing tools? +
Kazozo integrates with any major CRM, ecommerce platform, or business tool via API. We support Salesforce, HubSpot, Stripe, Shopify, Slack, and hundreds more. Custom integrations are available for enterprise requirements.
How does Kazozo handle data privacy and compliance? +
Kazozo is SOC 2 Type II certified and GDPR, CCPA, and HIPAA compliant. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We offer industry-specific compliance packages, and your data never leaves your designated region unless explicitly configured otherwise.
