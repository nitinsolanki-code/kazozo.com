It integrates with Google My Business, Yelp, Facebook, and other review platforms via their APIs. It also connects to your CRM to send review requests at the right moment and logs responses in your customer records.
Every interaction with the Review Manager counts as one AI response against your plan's monthly pool. Your plan includes a set number of AI responses per month (Starter: 500, Growth: 2,000, Pro: 6,000). Overages are billed at your plan's per-AI-response rate.
