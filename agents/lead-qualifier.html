FAQ

Frequently asked questions

The Lead Qualifier ingests inbound leads from your website, email, or CRM, researches them across 6+ data providers, scores them using BANT criteria, calculates affluence and intent signals, and routes hot prospects to your sales team in seconds. No manual lead review.
It integrates directly with Salesforce, HubSpot, Pipedrive, and other CRM platforms. Leads are enriched and scored in real-time, then automatically assigned to sales reps based on your routing rules. Results are logged to your CRM instantly.
Every interaction with the Lead Qualifier counts as one AI response against your plan's monthly pool. Your plan includes a set number of AI responses per month (Starter: 500, Growth: 2,000, Pro: 6,000). Overages are billed at your plan's per-AI-response rate.
Absolutely. You define your BANT criteria, scoring weights, affluence signals, routing rules, and which data providers to use. The Qualifier adapts to your sales process and ideal customer profile exactly.
If a lead lacks sufficient data or signals are inconclusive, the Lead Qualifier flags it as needing manual review and provides all research performed so far. Your sales team can then make the final call with full context.
