The Sales Agent runs personalized outreach campaigns, schedules follow-ups at optimal times, generates custom proposals, qualifies prospects, books meetings, and logs every touchpoint to your CRM. It turns lead lists into closed deals without manual busywork.
It integrates with Salesforce, HubSpot, Pipedrive, Outreach, and other CRM platforms. Prospects are pulled from your CRM, outreach is logged automatically, and new deals are created in your pipeline in real-time.
Every interaction with the Sales Agent counts as one AI response against your plan's monthly pool. Your plan includes a set number of AI responses per month (Starter: 500, Growth: 2,000, Pro: 6,000). Overages are billed at your plan's per-AI-response rate.
