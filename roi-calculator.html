How this is calculated

Leak = monthly conversations × missed % × 70% recoverable × close rate × deal value. We assume ~70% of missed leads are recoverable by an always-on agent; the rest fail for reasons no tool can fix (wrong number, tire-kickers, etc.). Treat the leak number as an upper bound of recoverable revenue — real-world results depend on your traffic quality and sales process.

AI response usage = conversations × responses per conversation. Each plan includes a response allowance; anything over bills at that plan's per-response rate (Starter $0.40, Growth $0.30, Pro $0.22).

Recommended plan is the plan with the lowest all-in cost (base + overage) for your usage level. Voice Agent is a separate add-on at $1.10 per answered call.

Why the 5-minute window matters

The longer a lead waits, the more the conversion curve collapses. It's the single highest-leverage thing most SMBs can fix.

5 min → 30 min

Conversion rates drop by roughly 10× when response time stretches past the first half hour. After-hours leads often wait until morning — if they're followed up at all.

Your team can't be on at 2am
