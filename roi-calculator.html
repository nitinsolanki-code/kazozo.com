How this is calculated

Leak = monthly leads × missed % × close rate × deal value. This is a directional number, not a contract — your actual results depend on your traffic quality, sales process, and how fast you staff your team today. Most SMBs recover 60–80% of the "missed" bucket by switching on an always-on AI agent, so treat the leak number as an upper bound of recoverable revenue. Why the 5-minute window matters

The longer a lead waits, the more the conversion curve collapses. It's the single highest-leverage thing most SMBs can fix.

5 min → 30 min

Conversion rates drop by roughly 10× when response time stretches past the first half hour. After-hours leads often wait until morning — if they're followed up at all.

Your team can't be on at 2am

But your competitors' AI can. Every lead that hits your site outside business hours is effectively gone unless something picks it up in the moment.
